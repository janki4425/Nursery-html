Types of plants

  1. Herbs
    2.
    • Small,soft stemmed plants
    • Usually live for one season
    • Examples:Basil,mint,spinach
  2. Shrubs
    3.
    • Medium-sized,woody plants with multiple stems
    • Shorter than trees
    • Examples:rose,hibiscus,jasmin
  3. Trees
    4.
    • Large,woody plants with single main stem or trunk
    • live for many years
    • Examples:Mango,Neem,Banyan
  4. Climbers
    5.
    • weak stem;need support to grow upwards
    • Examples:Grapevine,Money plants,Peas
  5. Creepers
    6.
    • Grow along the ground
    • Examples:Pumpkin,watermelon,strawberry
